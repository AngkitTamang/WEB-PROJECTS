Beautiful Monastery

Discover the tranquility and peace at the Beautiful Monastery. Nestled in the mountains, this monastery offers a serene environment for meditation and reflection. Visitors can explore the ancient architecture, participate in guided tours, and enjoy the breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Whether you are seeking spiritual enlightenment or simply a peaceful retreat, the Beautiful Monastery is the perfect destination. Come and experience the calm and serenity that this sacred place has to offer.

Tai Phake Buddhist Monastery

Ancient Monastery

Step back in time and explore the Ancient Monastery. This historic site offers a glimpse into the past with its well-preserved architecture and artifacts. Visitors can take guided tours to learn about the history and significance of the monastery, and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

The Ancient Monastery is a must-visit for history enthusiasts and those seeking a deeper understanding of the region's cultural heritage.

Modern Monastery

Experience the blend of tradition and modernity at the Modern Monastery. This contemporary site offers state-of-the-art facilities for meditation and spiritual practices, while maintaining the essence of monastic life. Visitors can participate in workshops, attend lectures, and enjoy the serene environment.

The Modern Monastery is perfect for those looking to explore new ways of spiritual growth and personal development.
